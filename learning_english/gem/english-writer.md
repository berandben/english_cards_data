My weekend plans

First, I usually wake up early on Saturday, at about half past seven. In the morning, I always listen to music and I think about what I need to do. I have already finished my homework, so I feel happy.

Before noon, I sometimes go to the supermarket with my mother. I wait for her in the car because she likes to shop alone. After that, we have lunch together at home. I normally eat soup or pasta.

In the afternoon, I am going to meet my friends at the park. We often play football there. Although I am not a good player, I enjoy running and laughing with them. Moreover, this weekend we plan to watch a movie at my friend's house.

When I arrive at his house, I will look at the TV and listen to his comments about the film. Finally, I never go to bed late on Sunday because I have school on Monday. However, I think this weekend will be fantastic.
